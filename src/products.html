Invoice Payment

  • Invoices can be used for paying the Registered Distributors by selecting the distributor-wise Invoices preuploaded on the Fundly App
  • Invoices can also be used for paying a lump–sum amount to the Registered Distributors on the Fundly App

InstaCash

  • Insta Cash can be used for paying the Non–Registered Distributors by getting the funds in the Business bank account of the Retailer
  • Borrower can use the funds to pay off the pending Invoices with their Distributors

Here's How the Fund Flow Works

Fund Request Flow

  • Pay Distributors – to pay due invoices or lump sum to the registered distributors
  • Insta Cash – to pay non-registered distributors or for personal use
  • Funds are transferred directly from the Lender's bank account to the Distributor's or Retailer's bank account according to the product selected

Repayment Flow

  • Payment Gateway – Fundly has integrated with PGs for providing in-app repayment facility through UPI/Net Banking
  • Funds are transferred directly from the Borrower's bank account to Lender's bank account
